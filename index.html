What Is Dripps?


Dripps is committed to creating a safe and secure enviorment for event organizers and attendees Our user-friendly dApp makes it easy for event organizers to create and manage their events, as well as allow attendeess to discover and participate in events that intrest them. Drips is distinct from other platforms because it allows event organizers to mint unique NFTS representing their events.

Our Mission

The team behind Dripps is on a mission to make creating and attending social gatherings more transparent, safe and cost effective for everyone. We aspire to change how events are organized and attended. Our inuitive dApp facilitates simple event management for planners and access to relevant events for participants. It is through the use of blockchain technology that Dripps is able to create this platform where event planners and attendees can benefit from increased safety, privacy and claity in any gathering they take part in.
